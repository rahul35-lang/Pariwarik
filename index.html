About Our Resort

A Gateway to the Wilderness

Nestled on the edge of Chitwan National Park, Pariwarik Hotel offers a perfect blend of modern luxury and traditional Tharu architecture.

Whether you are here for an adventurous jungle safari or a peaceful retreat by the Rapti river, our dedicated team ensures your stay is memorable. Experience the warmth of "Pariwarik" (Family) hospitality where every guest is treated like our own.
